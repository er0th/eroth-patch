Heroes of the Storm — Patch Notes — March 2026

Blizzard Entertainment  ·  March 2026

We've just deployed a major update to the live servers in order to apply balance changes and address known issues.

Quick Navigation

  • Balance Updates
  • Known Issues

Balance Updates

Eroth

Prince of the Radiant Bloodline

Base

Judgment [W]
  • Damage increased from 140 to 165.
  • Cooldown reduced from 10 seconds to 9 seconds.
Wake of Ashes [E]
  • Damage reduced from 310 to 280.

Talents

Level 1

Royal Confidence
  • Bonus Attack Damage reduced from 15% to 12%.
Unquestioned Rule
  • Now also increases Movement Speed by 10% while no enemies are nearby.

Level 7

Execution Protocol
  • Bonus damage against marked targets reduced from 40% to 30%.

Level 10 — Heroics

Execution Sentence [R1]
  • Delay reduced from 3 seconds to 2.5 seconds.
  • Damage increased from 360 to 400.
Final Verdict [R2]
  • Silence duration increased from 0.75 to 1 second.
  • Cooldown increased from 60 to 70 seconds.

Level 20

Unavoidable Destiny New Passive
  • This Talent is automatically activated at Level 20. Cannot be canceled, dispelled, or avoided.
  • Adds a permanent unit to the player's household.
  • Gaming Time reduced from 100% to 30%.
  • Sleep reduced from 6 hours to 3 hours.
  • Responsibility increased from 100% to 250%.
Developer Comment

Eroth has been performing strongly in coordinated environments. While we're happy with his ability to dominate chosen targets, we felt it was time to reinforce his… long-term scaling potential. The bloodline… grows stronger.

Known Issues

Known Issues
  • Player may experience random AFKs during night phases.
  • Increased frequency of unexpected voice interruptions.
  • Raid availability may be… situational.
